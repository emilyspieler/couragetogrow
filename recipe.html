Recipes and Meals

Healthy eating doesn't have to mean sacrificing taste. Here are some of our favorite (tried and tested) recipies to share!

Tex-Mex Tofu Scrambler:

  • 1 block firm tofu, pressed
  • drizzle of olive oil
  • 1/4 c sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 c cubed zucchini
  • 1/2 c cauliflower
  • 1/2 c green pepper, diced
  • 1/2 c diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 onion diced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tbsp nutritional yeast
  • salt and pepper for taste

Banana Pancakes:

  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 baking powder
  • 4 tbsp gluten free flower
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  1. Mash the ripe bananas until smooth
  2. Combine the eggs and the vanilla to the ripe bananas
  3. Fold in the gluten free flower and baking powder
  4. Measure 1/3 of the batter and pour into a hot griddle. Wait 2-3 minutes or until air bubbles show. Flip to other side for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Serve with warm maple syrup!

Paleo Banana Bread:

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 ripe bananas
  • 3/4 almond flower
  • 1/4 oat flower
  • 1/4 maple syrup
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 chopped dates
  • 1/4 golden raisins
  • 1/2 banana (to decorate the top)

Prep: Bake this by doing a lot of different things like blah blah blah

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mash bananas and mix with eggs and maple syrup.
  3. Measure flower by spooning into measuring cup
  4. Sift dry ingredients into wet mixture
  5. Pour into 9x5 baking dish and top with bananas. Bake for 45 minutes.

Vegan Banana Chia Seed Pudding

  • 1 1/2 blended banana
  • 2/3C Cashew Milk
  • 4 TBSP Chia Seeds
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • Chill in fridge overnight
  • Enjoy!

Avacado Toast

  • baked 1/2 inch slices of sweet potato (drizzled with a little evoo and salted) at 350 for 15 minutes
  • mash up 1 avacado, with a little onion, garlic, jalapeño, and salt & pepper
  • then add dill, a bit of lime juice and garlic powder
  • spread avocado mixture on top of sweet potato
  • top with beets, protein of choice (egg), diced tomatoes, salt & pepper and dill.

Kale with Imperfect Produce
